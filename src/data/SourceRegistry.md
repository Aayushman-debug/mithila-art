## Section 3: Artist Biographies — Reference Sources

### Sita Devi (1914–2005)
- Padma Shri citation: Government of India, 1981 (Ministry of Home Affairs)
- Shilp Guru citation: Ministry of Textiles, Development Commissioner (Handicrafts), 2006
- National Award citation: Ministry of Textiles, 1975
- V&A Museum collection records (London) — verifiable at collections.vam.ac.uk
- Musée du Quai Branly collection records (Paris)
- Bihar Ratna Samman, Government of Bihar, 1984

### Ganga Devi (1928–1991)
- Padma Shri citation: Government of India, 1984
- National Crafts Museum mural records, New Delhi
- Smithsonian Institution Festival of American Folklife documentation
- Sahapedia.org — "Ganga Devi" profile (url: sahapedia.org)
- "Ganga Devi: A Mithila Painter" — monograph documentation

### Jagdamba Devi (1901–1984)
- National Award citation: Ministry of Textiles, 1970 — FIRST Madhubani artist to receive this
- Padma Shri citation: Government of India, 1975 — FIRST Madhubani artist to receive this
- All India Handicrafts Board historical records (1960s)
- Documentation of natural pigment techniques — Handicrafts Board archive

### Mahasundari Devi (1922–2013)
- Padma Shri citation: Government of India, 2011
- Shilp Guru citation: Ministry of Textiles, 2007
- National Award citation: Ministry of Textiles, 1982
- Tulsi Samman: Government of Madhya Pradesh, 1995
- Sahapedia.org — "Mahasundari Devi" profile
- Mithila Hastashilp Kalakar Audyogki Sahyog Samiti records, Ranti

### Baua Devi (born 1940)
- Padma Shri citation: Government of India, 2017
- National Award citation: Ministry of Textiles, 1984
- "Magiciens de la Terre" exhibition catalogue — Centre Georges Pompidou, Paris, 1989 (editors: Jean-Hubert Martin, Mark Francis)
- Centre Pompidou archive records, Paris

### Dulari Devi (born 1968)
- Padma Shri citation: Government of India, 2021
- "Following My Paintbrush" — Dulari Devi with Vaijayanti Bhatt, Zubaan Books, New Delhi, 2011 (ISBN 978-81-89884-87-0)
- Mithila Art Institute records
- State Award, Government of Bihar, 2012

## Section 5: What Is NOT Used

The following image types are explicitly excluded from this website:
- ❌ Pinterest boards or repins
- ❌ AI-generated portraits or artwork
- ❌ WhatsApp or personal messenger images
- ❌ Unsourced blog images
- ❌ Images without verifiable license
- ❌ Placeholder images (picsum.photos, lorem picsum, etc.)
- ❌ Stock photos (Getty, Shutterstock, Unsplash) without proper licensing for cultural heritage use
